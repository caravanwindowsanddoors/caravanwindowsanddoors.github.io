Product Information

Our Slim-Line Windows have reduced sight lines and a back to front depth of 48mm. Perfect for narrower wall construction.

The slim-line odd leg section sits on the external face of the building, giving a coverage of 19mm on all sides. A 6mm foam tape can be installed on the window, which when compressed during installation stage, becomes watertight and fills in any gaps within the external cladding giving a clean finish.

The windows are installed by knocking an aluminium fixing bar into the specially designed window, giving a secure clamp fix finish. The fixing bar then doubles up as an internal trim which sits flush to the inner wall. This is suitable for wall construction of 38mm or 53mm. If your wall construction differs, then we can fix them using screws and trim up on the inside.

The Slim-line windows hold a 24mm double glazed unit, whilst the door accomodates a 28mm double glazed unit / panel.
